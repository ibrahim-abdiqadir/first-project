Welcome to Sunday

OUR MISSION

A better planet, one yard at a time

It started with two brothers and one radical idea: Home and lawn care
should be better. Better for you, better for your family, and better for
the planet.

CULTURE

Help us change lawn care
by doing what you love

At Sunday, we work hard to cultivate an
engaging, open atmosphere where our
diverse team can connect, share ideas, and
reimagine the American backyard. Our
culture reflects our commitment to
inclusion, individual growth, and curiosity.
Together we are building a brand that
empowers everyone to grow better.

The only thing missing is you!

SUSTAINABILITY

Our commitment to the planet

Sunday uses cutting-edge plant science
and simple ingredients to formulate all of
our products. From lawn and garden
fertilizers to pest and weed control, we
design everything with the full ecosystem in
mind—getting results without all the toxic
stuff.

We’re a 1% for the Planet business, giving a
portion of every sale to help preserve
wildlife habitats across the country.
